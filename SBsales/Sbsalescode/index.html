Refractory Bed Material

High-quality heat-resistant material for boiler use.

Refractory bed materials are high-temperature materials used in industrial processes that require resistance to extreme heat, chemical corrosion, and erosion. They come in different forms and are made from various materials. Advantages include high-temperature resistance, chemical resistance, durability, and thermal shock resistance. Refractory bed materials are used in various industries such as steel production, textile industries, paper industries, glass manufacturing, cement production, petrochemical processing, power generation, foundries, and waste incineration.

Key Features of Refractory Bed Materials

  • Thermal-shock-resistant
  • High-strength
  • Stable
  • Non-fouling
  • Low-porosity
  • Customizable-size
  • Dense
  • Chemically-stable

Boiler Water Treatment Chemicals

Protect your boiler with our high-quality water treatment chemicals.

Boiler water treatment chemicals are used to prevent corrosion, scale formation, and microbiological growth in boilers and associated equipment. These chemicals are added to the boiler water to maintain the desired water chemistry and prevent the formation of harmful deposits. Common boiler water treatment chemicals include oxygen scavengers, scale inhibitors, pH adjusters, and biocides. Effective use of these chemicals can improve boiler efficiency, reduce maintenance costs, and extend the life of the equipment.

Key Features of Boiler Water Treatment Chemicals

  • Corrosion-inhibiting
  • Scale-inhibiting
  • Alkalinity-adjusting
  • Oxygen-scavenging
  • Biocidal
  • Dispersant
  • pH-stabilizing
  • Cleaning.

String Wound Filter Cartridges

Efficient and Cost-Effective Filtration with String Wound Filter Cartridges

String wound filter cartridges are a type of depth filter that provide efficient and cost-effective filtration for a wide range of applications, including water treatment, chemical processing, and oil and gas production. The cartridges are made by winding layers of high-quality fibers around a central core, which creates a tortuous path for fluid flow and removes contaminants from the fluid. They are available in different sizes, materials, and micron ratings to suit various filtration needs.

Industrial Thinners

Efficient solvent solutions for industrial coatings and adhesives

Industrial thinners are solvents used to thin and clean various types of industrial coatings, adhesives, and other chemical products. They are typically formulated to dissolve, dilute, or reduce the viscosity of paints, varnishes, and other coatings, allowing for smoother application and a better finish. Industrial thinners are commonly used in industrial applications such as automotive refinishing, metalworking, and surface preparation. They are available in a variety of formulations and chemical compositions to suit different applications and requirements.

Protect and Customize Your Products with Corrugated Boxes

Corrugated boxes are a popular type of packaging that are widely used for shipping and storage of products.
